About Us

Inturnstream is a dynamic software company focused on bridging the gap between education and industry. We believe in the power of hands-on experience and are committed to offering comprehensive internship programs that foster growth, innovation, and career development.
"Built on friendship and fueled by a shared passion for empowering students, Amigo Crew TechSol connects aspiring talent with real-world opportunities. We believe in the power of collaboration and offer virtual internships that bridge the gap between academia and industry.

At Inturnstream, our mission is simple: to ensure that every student, regardless of their background or the size of their college, has access to high-quality internship opportunities. We understand the challenges that students, especially those from smaller colleges, face in securing internships. Our founders, themselves graduates of a small college, experienced firsthand the frustration of limited access to real-world experience.
They faced rejection after rejection, feeling lost and uncertain about their future. This personal struggle ignited a passion to create a platform that would empower students with the skills and connections they need to succeed.
Inturnstream is more than just an internship platform; it's a community built on empathy and a shared commitment to student success. We connect aspiring talent with meaningful virtual internships that provide hands-on experience, industry exposure, and valuable mentorship.
Our goal is to break down barriers, level the playing field, and ensure that every student has the opportunity to thrive.
Join us on our mission to empower the next generation of leaders. Explore our internship opportunities and discover how Inturnstream can help you launch your career.
